Attention vs Connection

Digital community
captures attention.
Real-world community
compounds connection.

A digital community business gets bigger when more people look. A real-world community business gets more valuable when the right people spend meaningful time together.

This is not a content graph. It is a live relationship graph.

&
In real-world community, people are both the product and the customers.
Attention business One-to-many reach. More eyes. More minutes.
Connection business Many-to-many value. Better rooms. Stronger returns.
Attention narrows toward a platform.
Connection expands across the people in the room.
